Frequently Asked Questions

To request leave, navigate to the Leave Management section in your dashboard. Select the type of leave you need and fill out the form to submit your request.

You can update your profile details in the Profile section of the dashboard. Click "Edit" next to your profile information and make changes as needed.

To access your pay slips, go to the Payroll & Compensation section. You can view and download your pay slips from there.

Need Further Assistance?

If you are unable to find the information you're looking for, feel free to reach out to our support team.
